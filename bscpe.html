BS Computer Engineering

The BS Computer Engineering program focuses on hardware conceptualization, design and assembly, strengthened with the utilization of CISCO networking technology and certification. Students are trained to be experts in areas of computer architecture, electrical circuits and devices, communication systems and hardware design.

MISSION

The Department Of Computer Engineering is dedicated to:
1.Provide the skills, knowledge and attitudes that will allow our graduates to succeed as engineers, team player and leaders

2.Maintain Vital,State-Of-The Art Laborotory equipment and facilities which provides its students and faculty with opportunities to create,interpret, apply and disseminate knowledge

3.Prepare our graduates ofr life long learning to meet intellectual and career challenges

4.Contribute to the local, national and global growth of the community through the dedication of the core values of De la Salle Lipa


VISION

To be a leader in innovation and excellence in the field of Computer Engineering
