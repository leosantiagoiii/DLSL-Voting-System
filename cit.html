Certificate in Information Technology

The Certificate in Information Technology program provides students with the skills and knowledge needed for employment in entry-level positions and to pursue careers in programming, systems analysis, networking, and technical support in small organizations. It is also designed to act as a bridge to higher-level computing qualifications and provides with a solid foundation to enter a career in computing.
